About Me

This is it.

I am Patrick Joseph B. Ramos and this is my output for our Computer Science 3 project.

I used the Clean Blog Theme template from the bootstrap starter website as the framework of my articles, game, gallery, and archive. I changed some things, and added some little revisions of my own.

I created the home webpage myself, and I had put a lot of great effort into it. Unfortunately, it did not turn out the way I want it to. But what it is now is already satisfying for me. The homepage actually gets disrupted when viewed from a device smaller than a desktop computer screen. Viewing the homepage on notebook laptops would disrupt it, sadly.

Tremendous effort was given to this project, even though this was actually crammed and all. I would accept any grade given for this project, especially I feel that I had not given it my all and that I had not done my best. I do know that I had done enough, though. But in all honesty, that "enough" was kind of not enough, for me, anyway.
