  1. What is the Internet of Things?

       The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physiological objects embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and network connectivity that enables these objects to collect and observe data.

       The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physiological objects embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and network connectivity that enables these objects to collect and exchange data.

       The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physical objects embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and network connectivity that enables these objects to collect and exchange data.

       The Internet of Things (IoT) is the group of physical objects embedded with electronics, software, sensors, and network connectivity that enables these objects to collect and exchange data.
